SECTION 2: OFFICE ACCESS & ID CARD
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1 ID CARD & ACCESS CARD
───────────────────────────
  • Company ID card issued on Day 1 (collect from Admin Desk)
  • ID card must be worn visibly at all times inside office premises
  • Access card grants entry to building and floor (role-based access)
  • Working hours access   : All employees (8:00 AM – 10:00 PM)
  • After-hours access     : Manager approval required (submit via Facilities Portal)
  • Weekend access         : Prior approval from Department Head + Admin

2.2 LOST OR DAMAGED ID / ACCESS CARD
───────────────────────────────────────
  1. Report immediately to Admin Desk (Ground Floor) or facilities@hexaware.com
  2. Temporary access card issued same day (valid 3 days)
  3. Replacement card processed within 2 working days
  4. Charges:
     - First replacement : Free
     - Second replacement: INR 200
     - Third and beyond  : INR 500 + HR notification

2.3 VISITOR MANAGEMENT
────────────────────────
  • All visitors must be pre-registered by employee host
  • Register visitor: Facilities Portal → Visitor Management → Add Visitor
  • Visitor receives email with QR code for entry
  • Visitors must show valid government photo ID at reception
  • Visitor badge issued at reception — must be returned on exit
  • Visitors not allowed beyond designated visitor areas without escort
  • Visitor access after 7:00 PM requires Senior Manager approval

2.4 CONTRACTOR & VENDOR ACCESS
────────────────────────────────
  • All contractors must be pre-approved by Facilities + IT + HR
  • Escorted by Hexaware employee at all times
  • Contractor badge issued daily (not overnight)
  • Background verification required for all long-term contractors

  Dinner             : 7:30 PM – 9:30 PM (for employees working late)

3.2 MEAL SUBSIDY
──────────────────
  • Subsidized meals available for all employees
  • Meal allowance of INR 1,500/month auto-credited with salary
  • Cafeteria meal prices are subsidized (50–60% below market rate)
  • Payment: Company cafeteria card (loaded with monthly allowance)
    or UPI / cash accepted
  • How to get cafeteria card: Admin Desk → Cafeteria Card Request

3.3 MENU & DIETARY OPTIONS
────────────────────────────
  • Daily rotating menu — posted on intranet by 9:00 AM each day
  • Vegetarian and non-vegetarian options available daily
  • Vegan options available on request (inform cafeteria staff)
  • Jain food available on request (24 hours advance notice)
  • Allergen information available at serving counter
  • Special dietary needs: email facilities@hexaware.com

3.4 CAFETERIA RULES
─────────────────────
  • Keep the cafeteria clean — dispose waste in designated bins
  • Do not waste food
  • Outside food delivery (Swiggy/Zomato) allowed at reception only
    (not inside cafeteria or workfloor)
  • Formal attire not required in cafeteria
  • No loud music or disruptive behavior
  • Outside guests in cafeteria require prior approval from Admin

SECTION 4: TRANSPORT & COMMUTE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 COMPANY TRANSPORT (BUS / SHUTTLE)
───────────────────────────────────────
  • Company-operated shuttle available for eligible employees
  • Routes cover major residential areas near each office location
  • Route details and timings: Facilities Portal → Transport → My Route
  • Registration required: Facilities Portal → Transport → Register

  Pickup timings (morning)  : 7:30 AM – 9:00 AM (location-dependent)
  Drop timings (evening)    : 6:30 PM – 8:00 PM
  Night shift drop          : Available for employees working past 9:00 PM
  Weekend shuttle           : Limited routes — check portal for schedule

4.2 TRANSPORT ELIGIBILITY
───────────────────────────
  • All confirmed employees are eligible for company transport
  • Probationary employees eligible from Day 1
  • Transport is free of charge for all registered employees
  • Employees must register a minimum 3 days before first use

4.3 CAB POLICY (FOR OFFICIAL LATE WORKING)
────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Employees working past 9:30 PM entitled to company-arranged cab
  • Female employees: entitlement from 7:00 PM onwards
  • Request cab: Facilities Portal → Transport → Cab Request
  • Submit by 8:00 PM for same-night use
  • Direct to registered home address only (no detours)
  • Pre-approved vendor cabs only (Ola Corporate, Meru)
  • Personal cab bookings reimbursed only if company cab unavailable
    (attach receipt and notify facilities same night)

4.4 PARKING
────────────
  Office Parking (4-wheelers):
  • Parking slots allocated on first-come, first-served basis
  • Registration required: Facilities Portal → Parking → Register Vehicle
  • Monthly parking sticker issued on registration
  • Unregistered vehicles not allowed in basement parking
  • No overnight parking without special permission from Admin

  Two-wheeler Parking:
  • Dedicated two-wheeler zone — Ground Floor entrance, Wing B
  • No registration required — parking sticker issued on Day 1
  • Helmets must be worn on premises (company rule)

  Visitor Parking:
  • Marked visitor bays at main entrance (max 4 hours)
  • Visitors must register vehicle at reception

SECTION 5: MEETING ROOMS & CONFERENCE FACILITIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5.1 MEETING ROOM BOOKING
──────────────────────────
  • All meeting rooms must be booked in advance
  • Booking: Microsoft Outlook → Calendar → New Meeting → Add Room
    Or: Facilities Portal → Meeting Rooms → Book Room
  • Rooms available: 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM (Monday – Saturday)
  • Maximum booking duration: 4 hours per slot (extendable if available)
  • Book minimum 30 minutes in advance
  • Cancel booking if not needed (to free up room for others)

5.2 MEETING ROOM TYPES
────────────────────────
  Room Type           Capacity    Floor    Facilities
  ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Huddle Rooms        2–4         All      TV screen, whiteboard
  Small Meeting Room  6–8         2, 4, 6  Projector, video conf
  Large Meeting Room  10–15       3, 7     Dual screen, video conf
  Board Room          20–30       10       Full AV, video conf, catering
  Training Room       30–50       1        Projector, flip charts, PA system
  Town Hall / Auditorium 200+     Ground   Full AV, stage, recording

5.3 MEETING ROOM RULES
────────────────────────
  • Vacate room 5 minutes before booking ends
  • Do not extend booking without releasing current slot first
  • Leave room clean — put chairs back, erase whiteboard
  • Report AV issues to Facilities immediately
  • No food allowed in Board Room (beverages only)
  • External visitors in meeting rooms must be registered

5.4 VIDEO CONFERENCING SETUP
──────────────────────────────
  • All large meeting rooms equipped with:
    - Microsoft Teams Rooms
    - Logitech Rally camera + speaker bar
    - Dual display screens
  • For support with video conferencing setup: IT Help Desk
  • Test AV equipment 15 minutes before important meetings

5.5 CATERING FOR MEETINGS
───────────────────────────
  • Catering available for meetings with 10+ participants
  • Request via Facilities Portal → Catering Request
  • Minimum 24 hours advance notice required
  • Options: tea/coffee service, snack trays, lunch boxes
  • Client meetings: Premium catering available (manager approval)
  • Budget: Standard catering charged to department cost center

SECTION 7: HEALTH, SAFETY & EMERGENCY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

7.1 FIRST AID
──────────────
  • First aid kits available on every floor (near fire exit)
  • Trained first aiders present in each building during working hours
  • First aider list: posted on every floor notice board
  • Medical room: Ground Floor, Building A (nurse available 9 AM – 6 PM)
  • Ambulance on call: 1800-XXX-XXXX (Facilities Emergency Line)

7.2 FIRE SAFETY
────────────────
  • Fire exits marked on every floor — know your nearest exit
  • Fire drills conducted twice a year (all employees must participate)
  • Do not block fire exits or emergency corridors
  • Fire extinguishers on every floor — do not tamper with them
  • In case of fire:
    1. Activate nearest fire alarm
    2. Call security: 1800-XXX-XXXX
    3. Evacuate using nearest staircase (do NOT use lift)
    4. Assemble at designated muster point (parking lot, Gate 2)
    5. Do not re-enter until all-clear given by Fire Warden

7.3 EMERGENCY EVACUATION
──────────────────────────
  Muster Point       : Main parking area, Gate 2
  Floor Wardens      : Posted on each floor notice board
  Emergency numbers  :
    Security Control Room : Ext. 100
    Medical Emergency     : Ext. 101
    Fire                  : Ext. 102
    Police                : 100
    Ambulance             : 108

7.4 OFFICE SAFETY RULES
─────────────────────────
  • No smoking anywhere inside or within 10 meters of building
  • Smoking zone: designated area near Gate 2 exit only
  • No cooking or use of personal electrical appliances at desk
  • Report any suspicious person or package to security immediately
  • Wet floor / hazard: report to Facilities immediately
  • Electrical issues: do not attempt self-repair — call IT or Facilities

SECTION 12: TOP 20 GENERAL & FACILITIES FAQs
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q1.  I lost my access card. What do I do?
A:   Report to Admin Desk immediately. Temporary card issued same day.
     Replacement card ready in 2 working days. First replacement is free.

Q2.  How do I register for company transport?
A:   Facilities Portal → Transport → Register.
     Register at least 3 days before first use.

Q3.  I am working late. How do I get a cab home?
A:   Facilities Portal → Transport → Cab Request.
     Submit by 8:00 PM. Female employees eligible from 7:00 PM onwards.

Q4.  How do I book a meeting room?
A:   Microsoft Outlook → New Meeting → Add Room.
     Or Facilities Portal → Meeting Rooms → Book Room.
     Book minimum 30 minutes in advance.

Q5.  The AC in my area is too cold/hot. Who do I contact?
A:   Log a ticket on Facilities Portal or email facilities@hexaware.com.
     Response within 2 hours during working hours.

Q6.  What are the cafeteria timings?
A:   Breakfast 8–10 AM, Lunch 12:30–2:30 PM, Evening snacks 4:30–5:30 PM,
     Dinner 7:30–9:30 PM (for late workers).

Q7.  Can I get food delivered to the office?
A:   Yes, Swiggy/Zomato deliveries accepted at reception only.
     Not allowed directly to workfloor or cafeteria.

Q8.  How do I register my vehicle for parking?
A:   Facilities Portal → Parking → Register Vehicle.
     Monthly parking sticker issued upon registration.

Q9.  How do I register for the office gym?
A:   Facilities Portal → Gym → Register.
     Free for all confirmed employees. Timings: 6 AM – 10 PM.

Q10. I need stationery for my project. How do I request it?
A:   Facilities Portal → Stationery → Request.
     For bulk orders give 2 days advance notice.

Q11. How do I invite a visitor to the office?
A:   Facilities Portal → Visitor Management → Add Visitor.
     Visitor gets QR code on email. They must show government photo ID.

Q12. Where is the nearest fire exit from my floor?
A:   Fire exits are clearly marked on every floor.
     Check the emergency evacuation map on your floor notice board.

Q13. The washroom on my floor has an issue. Who do I report to?
A:   Log on Facilities Portal or call Facilities Desk.
     Resolved within 4 hours during working hours.

Q14. Can I bring my personal laptop to office?
A:   Yes for personal use only. Company data must not be accessed
     or stored on personal devices. Must connect to HEXAWARE_GUEST
     Wi-Fi only (not corporate network).

Q15. How do I request a height-adjustable desk or ergonomic equipment?
A:   Facilities Portal → Ergonomics Request.
     Available on request — medical need prioritized.

Q16. Is there a smoking area in the office?
A:   Yes, designated smoking zone near Gate 2 exit only.
     Smoking anywhere else on premises is strictly prohibited.

Q17. I want to organize a team event. How do I get support?
A:   Email facilities@hexaware.com with team size, date, and type of event.
     Budget up to INR 1,000 per person per quarter with manager approval.

Q18. The printer on my floor is not working. Who do I contact?
A:   Raise an IT ticket on IT Portal for printer issues.
     For paper/toner refill contact Facilities Desk.

Q19. Are there lockers available for employees?
A:   Yes, lockers available near gym area and on select floors.
     Request via Facilities Portal → Locker Request.
     Lockers are shared (gym lockers) or personal (floor lockers, limited).

Q20. Who do I call in case of a medical emergency in the office?
A:   Call Ext. 101 (Medical Emergency) or 1800-XXX-XXXX immediately.
     First aiders are present on every floor during working hours.
     Medical room is on Ground Floor, Building A.
