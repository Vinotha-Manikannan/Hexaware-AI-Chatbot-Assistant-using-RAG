SECTION 2: ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
================================================================

Employees can claim reimbursement for:
  ✓ Business travel (flights, trains, cabs — economy class)
  ✓ Client entertainment meals (with business justification)
  ✓ Hotel stays (within approved grade limits)
  ✓ Official mobile and internet bills (WFH employees)
  ✓ Parking fees during official travel
  ✓ Office supplies purchased for official use
  ✓ Certification and training fees (pre-approved only)
  ✓ Medical expenses (as per insurance limits)

NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
  ✗ Alcohol or personal entertainment
  ✗ Personal travel or tourism
  ✗ Fines, penalties, or traffic challans
  ✗ Unapproved software or subscriptions
  ✗ Expenses without valid bills or invoices
  ✗ Claims submitted after 30-day deadline
  ✗ Unapproved flight upgrades

SECTION 3: HOW TO SUBMIT AN EXPENSE CLAIM
================================================================

STEP BY STEP PROCESS
  Step 1 : Collect all original bills and digital invoices
  Step 2 : Login to Finance Portal: https://financeportal.hexaware.com
  Step 3 : Go to Claims → New Expense Claim
  Step 4 : Fill in: date, category, amount, description, project code
  Step 5 : Upload bill (PDF format, maximum 5 MB per file)
  Step 6 : Click Submit for Manager Approval
  Step 7 : Manager approves within 2 working days
  Step 8 : Finance reviews and processes within 3 working days
  Step 9 : Payment credited to salary account within 10 business days

CLAIM SUBMISSION RULES
  - All claims must be submitted within 30 days of expense date
  - Claims older than 30 days will be REJECTED without exception
  - Bills must be in employee's name or company name
  - Duplicate claims (same bill twice) are flagged and reported to HR
  - False or inflated claims result in disciplinary action

SECTION 5: TRAVEL POLICY
================================================================

DOMESTIC TRAVEL
  - All domestic travel must be booked via company Travel Desk
  - Travel Portal: https://travel.hexaware.com
  - Book minimum 3 days in advance for best fares
  - Booking within 24 hours requires manager + Finance approval
  - Flight: Economy class only (all grades)
  - Train: 3AC or above
  - Local cab: Ola/Uber app-generated bills accepted

INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL
  - Requires pre-approval: Department Head + HR + CFO
  - Submit International Travel Request on Finance Portal minimum 7 days in advance
  - Visa and travel insurance arranged by company Travel Desk

  Per Diem Rates 2025:
  USA              : USD 180/day
  Europe           : EUR 150/day
  Singapore        : SGD 280/day
  Japan            : JPY 18,000/day
  China            : CNY 1,200/day
  Middle East      : USD 120/day
  Southeast Asia   : USD 100/day

  Flight class:
  - Less than 6 hours     : Economy class (all grades)
  - 6 hours or more       : Business class (Manager grade and above only)

TRAVEL ADVANCE
  - Available for domestic and international trips
  - Request via Finance Portal → Travel Advance → submit with trip details
  - Requires manager + Finance approval
  - Must be settled within 10 days of trip completion
  - Unused advance must be returned immediately

TRAVEL EXPENSE SETTLEMENT DOCUMENTS
  Required after travel:
  ✓ Boarding passes (both directions)
  ✓ Hotel invoice with GST number
  ✓ Cab and taxi receipts
  ✓ Meal receipts above INR 500
  Submit via Finance Portal within 10 days of return

Cashless Hospitalization (network hospital):
  1. Show insurance e-card at hospital
     (Finance Portal → Insurance → E-card → Download)
  2. Hospital processes cashless directly with TPA
  3. No out-of-pocket payment required

Reimbursement (non-network hospital):
  1. Pay hospital bills
  2. Collect original bills, discharge summary, prescription
  3. Finance Portal → Claims → Medical Reimbursement
  4. Submit within 30 days of discharge
  5. Processing time: 15-21 working days

SECTION 9: EXPENSE CLAIMS FAQs
================================================================

Q: How do I submit an expense claim?
A: Finance Portal → Claims → New Expense Claim.
   Fill in date, category, amount, description.
   Upload bills in PDF format, then submit for manager approval.
   Claims must be submitted within 30 days of expense date.

Q: How do I submit an expense reimbursement?
A: Login to https://financeportal.hexaware.com
   Go to Claims → New Expense Claim → fill details → upload bills → submit.
   Payment credited within 10 business days after Finance approval.

Q: How long does reimbursement take?
A: Finance reviews within 3 working days of manager approval.
   Payment credited within 10 business days of Finance approval.

Q: My reimbursement claim was rejected. What do I do?
A: Check rejection reason in your email or Finance Portal → My Claims.
   Correct the issue and resubmit, or email claims@hexaware.com.

Q: I lost my travel bill. Can I still claim?
A: Contact your manager for a declaration note.
   Finance reviews on a case-by-case basis. Original bills strongly preferred.

Q: What expenses are NOT reimbursable?
A: Alcohol, personal travel, fines, expenses without bills,
   and any claim submitted after 30 days of expense date.

Q: Can I use Ola or Uber for official travel?
A: Yes. Ola and Uber app-generated bills are accepted.
   Attach PDF receipt from the app when submitting claim.

Q: What is the hotel limit for domestic travel?
A: Junior: INR 2,500/night | Senior: INR 3,500/night | Manager+: INR 6,000/night

Q: How do I check my claim status?
A: Finance Portal → My Claims → view current status of all submitted claims.

Q: How do I get reimbursed for my internet bill while working from home?
A: Finance Portal → Claims → Communication Allowance.
   Upload bill, submit by 20th of each month. Maximum INR 1,200 per month.
