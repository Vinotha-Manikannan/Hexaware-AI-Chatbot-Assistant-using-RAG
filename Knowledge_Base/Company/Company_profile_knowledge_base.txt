SECTION 1: COMPANY OVERVIEW
================================================================

Hexaware Technologies is a global AI-first digital and technology
services company. Founded in 1990 by Atul Nishar, Hexaware helps
enterprises worldwide accelerate their digital transformation journeys
through cloud, data, artificial intelligence, automation, and
software engineering.

Hexaware serves Fortune 500 companies and leading enterprises across
banking, healthcare, insurance, travel, manufacturing, and retail.
The company is known for combining deep industry expertise with
cutting-edge technology to deliver measurable business outcomes.

In 2021, Hexaware was taken private by Carlyle Group, one of the
world's largest private equity firms, and has since accelerated its
AI-first strategy and global expansion.
