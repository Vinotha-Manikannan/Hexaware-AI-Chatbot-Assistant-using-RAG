SECTION 1: LEAVE POLICY

Annual Leave (EL): 18 days per year
  - Carry forward: Maximum 30 days at year end
  - Leave encashment: Allowed for unused EL above 30 days
  - Cannot be used during probation period

Sick Leave (SL): 12 days per year
  - No carry forward — lapses at year end
  - Medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive days

Casual Leave (CL): 10 days per year
  - Maximum 3 consecutive days at a time
  - Cannot be carried forward or encashed
  - Minimum 1 day prior notice required

Maternity Leave: 26 weeks (182 days) for first two children
  - 12 weeks for third child onwards
  - Full pay during entire maternity leave period

Paternity Leave: 15 working days
  - Within 6 months of child birth or adoption
  - Full pay during leave period

Bereavement Leave: 5 working days
  - Immediate family: parents, spouse, children, siblings
  - 2 days for grandparents or in-laws

Marriage Leave: 3 days (once in service, on own marriage)

Unpaid Leave: Subject to Manager and HR approval

HOW TO APPLY FOR LEAVE
  1. Log into HR Portal: https://hrportal.hexaware.com
  2. Go to Leave Management → Apply Leave
  3. Select leave type, dates, and reason
  4. Submit for manager approval
  5. Email confirmation sent once approved or rejected
  6. For emergency leave: inform manager first, apply on portal within 2 working days

LEAVE BALANCE CHECK
  - HR Portal → Leave Balance
  - Also shown on monthly payslip
  - For discrepancies contact: hr@hexaware.com

SECTION 2: WORK FROM HOME POLICY
================================================================

  - Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week
  - Prior approval from Reporting Manager is required
  - WFH requests submitted through HR Portal
  - Employees must remain available during official working hours
  - Company security policies must be followed while working remotely
  - WFH on Monday and Friday requires prior approval
  - Not applicable during probation period
  - Extended WFH beyond 2 days requires HR and Business Head approval

HOW TO APPLY FOR WFH
  1. HR Portal → WFH Request
  2. Submit minimum 1 day in advance
  3. Manager approves or rejects within 4 hours
  4. Confirmation sent via email

SECTION 4: PROBATION POLICY
================================================================

Probation Period : 6 months from date of joining
Extension        : Up to 3 additional months with written notice

During probation employees are NOT eligible for:
  - Annual leave usage (accrues but cannot be used)
  - Work from home
  - Internal job transfers
  - Flexible timing

Probation Review Process:
  1. Manager submits review form 15 days before end date
  2. HR schedules formal review meeting
  3. Outcomes: Confirmed / Extended / Terminated

Confirmation: Letter issued by HR within 5 working days of review

NOTICE PERIOD
  During probation   : 1 month (employee) / 1 month (company)
  After confirmation : 2 months (employee) / 2 months (company)
  Senior roles (M5+) : 3 months (both parties)
  Notice period can be bought out with mutual consent

SECTION 9: SEPARATION & EXIT PROCESS
================================================================

RESIGNATION PROCESS
  1. Submit resignation via HR Portal → Resignation → Submit Letter
  2. Notice period starts from next working day
  3. Manager acknowledges within 2 working days
  4. HR sends exit checklist and schedules exit interview
  5. Complete knowledge transfer before last day
  6. Return all company assets on last working day

EXIT CHECKLIST
  ✓ Return laptop, access card, and all IT equipment
  ✓ Complete knowledge transfer document
  ✓ Clear all pending leaves or encash as per policy
  ✓ Settle all dues
  ✓ Attend exit interview with HR
  ✓ Obtain IT clearance certificate
  ✓ Obtain Finance clearance certificate

FULL AND FINAL SETTLEMENT
  - Processed within 45 days of last working day
  - Includes: unpaid salary, leave encashment, gratuity if eligible
  - Form 16 issued within statutory deadline
  - Relieving letter and experience letter issued within 30 days

SECTION 12: HR FAQs
================================================================

Q: How many leave days do I have?
A: Annual: 18 days, Sick: 12 days, Casual: 10 days.
   Check current balance on HR Portal → Leave Balance.

Q: Can I carry forward my unused leaves?
A: Only Annual Leave can be carried forward, up to 30 days maximum.
   Sick and Casual Leave lapse at year end and cannot be carried forward.

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: HR Portal → Leave Management → Apply Leave.
   Select leave type, dates, and reason, then submit for manager approval.

Q: How do I apply for WFH?
A: HR Portal → WFH Request → select date → manager approval required.
   Submit at least 1 day in advance. Maximum 2 days per week.

Q: What is the notice period after confirmation?
A: 2 months for most roles. 3 months for senior roles (M5 and above).
   Notice buyout possible with mutual consent.

Q: How do I resign from the company?
A: HR Portal → Resignation → Submit Letter.
   Notice period starts next working day. HR will send exit checklist.

Q: When will I receive my relieving letter after resignation?
A: Within 30 days of last working day, after all clearances completed.

Q: What is the appraisal process?
A: Self-appraisal in March, manager review by March 31,
   ratings finalized by April 30, increment letters issued in May.

Q: How do I report a POSH complaint?
A: Email posh@hexaware.com — strictly confidential.
   ICC will respond within 7 working days.

Q: What is the probation period?
A: 6 months. WFH and annual leave usage not permitted during probation.
